Services

Staffing

Keeping pace with projects and being on the look out for the extremely talented individuals with the right skill sets who can contribute to the success of the business has become ever more challenging. Here is where VsearchforU comes into play to bring the best suited talent for your company. We provide the best staffing service to fulfill the H.R related requirements and to reduce costs of employee administration. Our recruiters are exceptionally skilled in identifying top talent and matching them with the client’s needs.

Temporary Staffing

We provide reliable temporary staffing solutions that offer you the ability to build your staff strength without absorbing them into your company's payroll. This helps to assist overloaded employees during critical times, and keep projects moving.

Permanent Staffing

In today’s marketplace, it is very important to have the right talent in your team. Finding the right candidate for a permanent position, from reviewing resumes to identifying quality candidates, is time consuming and potentially costly. Our staffing solutions give you access to fully screened and qualified candidates in any industry. We have a strong team of consultants who can work on your company's staffing needs. We ensure that we offer our clients the best candidates, with the fastest turnaround time.

Training

Training is an integral part of human resource development programme in any Organization. Training makes employees more efficient and productive. Conducting training among employees expedite acquisition of the knowledge, skills, and abilities required for effective job performance. Having a trained workforce means your workers are learning new skills that can improve production, cut time spent in creation of your product (or service), reduce production costs, reduce mistakes, build confidence in your workforce, and create a better working environment. Our learning facility provide you with expert guidance, assess your training needs and customize training in line with market and Industry trends. Our consultants are highly skilled professionals with Industrial experience.
